## 4. One-time implementation & customization fee

### 4.1 Purpose

Customer engages Provider to perform **implementation and customization** work so Customer can use the Service in production. This work is **not** part of the monthly subscription and is priced **once** under this section and **Exhibit A**.

### 4.2 Founding fee

**One-time fee:** **USD $1,500.00** (founding customer rate; standard list reference **USD $2,500.00**).

### 4.3 Payment schedule

- **50%** (USD $750.00) due upon signing this agreement  
- **50%** (USD $750.00) due on **go-live** (subscription start date)

Provider is not required to mark go-live or enable production for daily field use until the go-live payment is received, except as both parties agree in writing.

### 4.4 Scope

Scope is defined in **Exhibit A — Statement of Work** attached to this agreement. Work outside Exhibit A is **out of scope** unless added via written change order.

### 4.5 Customer responsibilities

Customer will provide timely access to decision-makers, customer/site data, catalog information, and feedback needed to complete Exhibit A. Delays caused by missing Customer input may shift timelines without reducing fees already due.

### 4.6 Go-live and subscription start

**Go-live** means: production URL is available, Exhibit A deliverables are complete (or remaining items are documented as post-go-live with dates), and Customer has been given basic handoff for field and office use.

The **subscription start date** is the go-live date. Monthly fees under §3 begin on that date.

### 4.7 Change requests after Exhibit A

Work beyond Exhibit A or beyond included subscription support (§6) requires a **written change order** before Provider starts work:

- **Founding hourly rate:** USD **[125.00]** per hour (or fixed quote per change order)  
- **Standard hourly rate (reference):** USD **[175.00]** per hour  

Estimates over **four (4) hours** should be quoted as a fixed fee when possible.

### 4.8 Ownership of customization

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, customization and configuration performed under this agreement become part of Provider’s product and codebase. Provider may reuse general improvements for other customers. Customer retains ownership of **Customer business data** (see §7). Customer does not receive exclusive ownership of software code or generic workflows built for the Service.

## 5. Infrastructure

Provider operates and pays for **production hosting and standard deployment tooling** for Customer’s instance during the founding subscription term (including VPS, domain configuration as applicable, SSL, database, API process, and web application deploys).

**No separate hosting invoice** applies to Customer at the founding subscription rate.

Customer does not receive server administrator access unless separately agreed in writing.

**Third-party services** initiated or required by Customer beyond the standard deployment (for example paid SMS, payment processing, or accounting platform integrations) are **not included** in the one-time or monthly fees unless listed in Exhibit A or a change order. Pass-through costs are Customer’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed.

---

## 6. Ongoing support (subscription)

**Included** during the active subscription:

- Bug fixes and security patches for the Service  
- Routine production deploys of application updates  
- Minor configuration assistance up to **two (2) hours per calendar month** (combined phone, email, and remote support)

**Not included** in the subscription (requires change order per §4.7 or separate quote):

- New product modules (examples: billing/invoicing, QuickBooks or other accounting sync, dispatch/routing, native mobile apps, custom reporting)  
- Data migration beyond Exhibit A  
- On-site training beyond Exhibit A  
- Dedicated development sprints  

Unused support hours do not roll over unless Provider agrees in writing.

---

## 7. Data ownership and export

Customer retains ownership of Customer business data entered into the Service.

Provider hosts Customer data in Provider-managed systems during the term.

Upon termination or written request, Provider will make Customer data available for export in a reasonable format (for example CSV and/or database export) within **thirty (30) days**, subject to payment of outstanding fees (including any unpaid implementation balance).

Provider may retain backups for a limited period per Provider’s internal policy, then delete them.

---

## 8. Availability

The Service is provided on a **best-effort** basis at the founding tier. No uptime service-level agreement (SLA) applies unless added in a future written amendment.

Provider will use reasonable efforts to perform planned maintenance outside peak field hours when practicable and to provide notice when maintenance is expected to affect access.

---

## 9. Confidentiality

Each party will treat the other’s non-public business, financial, and technical information as confidential and will not disclose it except to employees, contractors, or advisors who need to know and are bound by similar obligations, or as required by law.

## 10. Renewal and step-up pricing (subscription)

After the initial twelve (12) month founding subscription term, the subscription renews month-to-month (or year-to-year if annual billing was elected) at Provider’s **then-current standard list price**, currently anticipated at **USD $149.00 per month** for a comparable two-user deployment, unless the parties sign a new written agreement.

**Optional founding loyalty discount:** If Customer remains in continuous good standing from the subscription start date, Customer receives **fifteen percent (15%) off** then-current standard list pricing after the founding term. Delete this sentence if not offered.

Either party may end subscription renewal after the minimum term with **thirty (30) days’ written notice**.

The one-time implementation fee is **not** recurring and is not refunded after go-live except as required by law or agreed in writing.

## 11. Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Provider’s total liability arising from this agreement is limited to **fees paid by Customer under this agreement in the twelve (12) months** preceding the claim (implementation + subscription). Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages (including lost profits) except where such limitation is prohibited by law.

The Service is business software; Customer is responsible for verifying operational and financial decisions made using exported or displayed data.
